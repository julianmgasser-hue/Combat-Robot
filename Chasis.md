# Chasis 

### Idea since 2026-06-05
To keep my Robot modular and easy to repair, I want to 
use a concept, I came up with. I call it the "Grillspies"
(german) aufbau. Like on a BBQ-Skewer, you can stick 
different modules on the bot. Two staffs hold the whole 
thing together and guarantee a structural stability. 
To fix the modules in place, I mount them with screws. 

In the middle are all the electronics. The whole bot is gonna be rather wide. 
On both sides, I'll add the Shuffler modules and on the outside, I'll add some 
flexible armour.

### Idea since 2026-06-16
I decided to ditch my "BBQ-Skewer" approach, because it's too heavy and doesn't really add value to the 
structure of the chasis, since most of the chasis is already one piece. It also allows me to have a lot more
space in the electronics part of my chasis. I still use the modules like shuffeling and sideguards, but it's fixed in place 
with screws. I hope, they don't have to take to much of the impacts.
