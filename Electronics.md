# Electronics

This is a list of all the electronic components i use in my combat robot.
The Stepdown is for the XIAO and the ELRS receiver, and the 5V1 Z-Diode in combination with two Capacitors ensures
the protection of the XIAO and ELRS receiver.

- Seed Studio XIAO ESP32S3
- BLDC Motor QA2205-1800KV 2s-3s
- ESC For BLDC Motor
- ELRS 2,4GHz NANO receiver
- 2x N20 Motor 6V 1000RPM
- DC-DC Buck Converter Step Down to 5V
- Silicon Cable: 26AWG
- 5V1 Z Diode
- 2x 100$100\ \mu\text{F} / 25\text{ V}$ capacitors (parallel, had no 220)
- 2s 350mAh LiPo Battery

A member of the ROFLS Discord friendly gave me some advice, for ex: That I should use 200-300mAh Batteries, instead of 550, since they are lighter, and i don't need the capacity anyways.
