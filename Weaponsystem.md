# Weaponsystem
I really want to have a unique weaponsystem. For this I came up with a 45° spinner. 
It's basicly a hybrid of vertical and horizontal spinner, but fixed in place at an 45° angle.

This is already giving me headaches, and I haven't even started with realising it. 

## Weaponblade
I decided to make a single tooth asymetric blade. This gives me a lot of bite. 
I went over a total of 3 iterations. 

### Iteration 1
I just drawed some lines in CAD and made it look like the single tooths, you know in the 
internet. It's center of mass was really close to the center of rotation. 
Generally speaking, every itearation has less than 0.1mm offset in X or Y achsis to the 
center of rotation. The problem was though, that the Blade was way to big, and its weight was
100g. 

### Iteration 2
I drasticly cut weight, while keeping the 5mm thickness. This also meant, that i had to cut 
some reacht, but i'm planning to compensate with groundgame and good driving. 
Iteration 2 already looked really smooth and weighted arround 20g. 

### Iteration 3
I only made some minor changes to Iteration 2. I rounded the edges that have to endure a lot 
of impact.
